Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 2 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup fresh milk
  • 1 piece salted duck egg sliced
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese
  • 3 pieces raw eggs
  • 1/4 cup grated coconut
  • Pre-cut banana leaf

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Mix rice flour, baking powder, and salt; set aside.
  3. Cream butter, add sugar, whisk, and mix in eggs.
  4. Combine dry mixture, then whisk in coconut milk and fresh milk.
  5. Line a baking pan with banana leaf, pour in batter, and bake for 15 minutes.
  6. Top with salted egg and grated cheese, then bake for another 15–20 minutes.
  7. Cool, brush with butter, top with grated coconut, and serve.

Tips

  • Banana leaves add aroma; ensure they're intact and pliable.
  • If unavailable, use parchment paper to line the pan.
  • To achieve charring, broil for 1–2 minutes after baking.
  • For deeper color, add yellow food coloring to the batter.
  • Top with sliced cream cheese or sharp cheddar for extra flavor.
